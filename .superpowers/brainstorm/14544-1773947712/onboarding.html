📋 Onboarding — Spec Notes
Steps
• Step 1: Profile (name, avatar, bio, location)
• Step 2: Interests (min 3 required)
• Step 3: Spaces (join recommended spaces)
• Step 4: Follow people (suggested based on interests)
Rules
• All steps have "Skip for now"
• Progress saved between steps (no data loss on back)
• Step 2 min 3 required (soft gate, can override)
• Admin can customize: enable/disable steps
Delivery
• Shown after first login (once)
• bn_users.onboarding_completed flag
• Can be re-triggered from profile settings
• Block: Onboarding (not forced — admin embeds)
