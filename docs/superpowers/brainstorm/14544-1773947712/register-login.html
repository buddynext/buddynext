📋 Register/Login — Spec Notes
Registration flow
• Social (Google/GitHub) or email
• Email requires OTP verification (6-digit)
• Username must be unique
• Admin can disable registration
Login flow
• Email + password
• Social OAuth (if configured)
• "Forgot password" → email reset link
• WordPress wp_login_form() backed
Delivery
• Block: Registration Form (embeddable anywhere)
• Block: Login Form (wraps wp_login_form)
• After register → Onboarding Wizard
• Social login: WP OAuth plugin hooks
