# BuddyBoss Feature Comparison

If you are evaluating a switch from BuddyBoss, this page maps the capabilities you rely on to where they live in BuddyNext. For each capability the table shows whether it is in BuddyNext Free, BuddyNext Pro, provided by an optional companion plugin, or coming soon.

We have kept this honest. Where something is provided by a companion plugin or is still on the way, we say so plainly rather than implying it ships in the core box.

## How to read the table

- **Free** - included in the free BuddyNext plugin, available the day you activate it.
- **Pro** - included in BuddyNext Pro, the paid upgrade.
- **Companion** - provided by a free, optional companion plugin you install when you want it. The companion is named in the notes.
- **Coming soon** - in active development for an upcoming release.

## Feature comparison

| Capability | Where it lives in BuddyNext | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Activity feed | Free | Text, link, and poll posts, with reactions, comments, share, bookmark, and report on every post. Photo and file posts are added by the WPMediaVerse companion. |
| Groups (called Spaces) | Free | Unlimited open and request-to-join spaces, each with its own feed, members, and optional tabs. Private (invite-only) and membership-gated spaces are Pro. |
| Profiles and profile fields | Free | Avatar, cover, bio, social links, and up to five custom fields with per-field privacy. Advanced field types (date, location, file, conditional) are Pro. |
| Member types | Free | Define categories like Student, Mentor, or Staff. Custom member labels such as Verified, Expert, or Staff badges are Pro. |
| Member directory | Free | Searchable, filterable directory that stays fast at scale. Member segments, tags, and CSV export are Pro. |
| Connections and Follows | Free | Both the mutual Connection model and the one-directional Follow model are included, along with block and mute. |
| Messaging | Companion | One-to-one direct messaging is powered by the free WPMediaVerse companion - message requests, mute, pin, archive, emoji reactions, and quoted replies. Read receipts, group messaging, and instant real-time delivery are Pro. |
| Media and albums | Companion | Profile media and albums, plus photo and file posts in the feed, are powered by the free WPMediaVerse companion, including private albums. |
| Forums (called Discussions) | Companion | Threaded, topic-based discussions are powered by the free Jetonomy companion and can appear as a forum tab inside a space. |
| Reactions | Free | Members react to posts, comments, and content. A custom reaction emoji set is Pro. |
| Notifications and email | Free | In-app notification bell and page, transactional emails (mention, reply, message, invite, and more), and per-user notification preferences. Broadcast campaigns, drip welcome sequences, digest emails, and open-rate stats are Pro. |
| Moderation | Free | Report button on all content, an admin review queue and moderation log, and actions to dismiss, remove, warn, strike, and suspend, including space-scoped moderation for space admins. Keyword blocklists, auto-action rules, spam scoring, bulk actions, blocklists, and an appeal system are Pro. |
| Memberships and monetization | Pro | Membership tiers, a paywall experience, and gated or tiered space access. |
| Gamification | Companion | Points, badges, and levels are provided by the free WB Gamification companion plugin. |
| Push and mobile | Pro | A native mobile app ships with Pro, and web push notifications are part of Pro. |
| Real-time updates | Free and Pro | The free plugin keeps feeds and counts current with regular background refreshes. Instant real-time delivery and typing indicators in messaging are Pro. |
| Analytics | Free and Pro | The free plugin shows basic admin counts. A full analytics dashboard (daily, weekly, and monthly active members, churn), space-level analytics, member self-analytics, and CSV export are Pro. |
| White-label | Pro | Available on the Unlimited (Agency) license tier of Pro. |
| Member and content importer from BuddyPress / BuddyBoss | Coming soon | A dedicated importer for bringing your existing community across is in active development for an upcoming release. See Moving Your Existing Members and Content. |

## Good to know

- **The free plugin is a complete community platform**, not a limited trial. Most of what a community needs to launch is in the Free column.
- **Companion plugins are free and optional.** Messaging, media, discussions, and gamification each come from a companion you add only when you want that capability. This keeps your core install lean.
- **Pro is additive.** Turning on Pro never removes or changes a free feature you already use - it only adds new capabilities.
- **License tier affects the number of sites, not the feature set**, with two exceptions: white-label is Unlimited-tier only, and the native mobile app ships with Pro.
