# What Changed for Members

> **For community owners:** This page is written for your members, not for you. Feel free to copy it into an announcement, an email, or a welcome post after you switch to BuddyNext, so people recognize the new names for the features they already know.

---

Welcome to our refreshed community. It is faster, cleaner, and easier to use - but a few things have new names and live in slightly new places. Here is a quick tour so you feel at home right away.

## Good news first

- **Your account still works.** Same login, same password. You do not need to sign up again.
- **You are still a member.** Nothing about your membership changed - just the experience around it.

## What has new names

- **Groups are now Spaces.** Spaces are the same idea - focused corners of the community for a topic, team, or interest. Browse the Spaces directory and join the ones you care about.
- **Friends are now Connections.** A Connection is the same two-way relationship: you send a request, the other person accepts, and you are connected. You can also simply Follow someone to keep up with them without a mutual request.
- **The activity stream is now the Feed.** It works the way you would expect - post, react, comment, share, and bookmark.

## Where things live now

- **Messages** are behind the messages icon in the header. Your private one-to-one conversations happen there.
- **Notifications** are behind the bell icon in the header, with a live count of anything new - new followers, replies, mentions, and more. You can choose which events notify you and how in your notification preferences.
- **Your profile** is yours to set up. Open it from the menu to add a photo, a cover image, a short bio, and your details.

## A couple of quick things to do

1. **Set up your profile again.** Add a real photo and a short bio - a filled-out profile gets more follows, more replies, and more connection accepts than a blank one.
2. **Join the spaces that matter to you.** Head to the Spaces directory and join your groups so your feed shows what you care about.
3. **Say hello.** Post something in the feed so people know you are here.

## Need a hand?

If something looks unfamiliar, give it a moment - most things are right where you would expect, just with a fresh look. If you get stuck, reach out to the community team and we will point you in the right direction.

Glad to have you here.
